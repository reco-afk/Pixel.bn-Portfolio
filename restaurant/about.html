About

A fictional kitchen with local roots.

Rumbia imagines a small evening restaurant in Bandar Seri Begawan. Its menu begins with Bruneian comfort food, then follows shared ingredients and cooking traditions across Southeast Asia.

Led by the ingredient.

The fictional kitchen team prepares sambals, spice pastes and broths in small batches. Open fire adds smoke without hiding the freshness underneath.

Browse the dishes ↗

Bright herbs. Deep fire.

Calamansi, torch ginger, pandan, turmeric and local greens form the centre of the menu. The compositions are contemporary, but the flavours remain recognisable.

Chef Amina Rahman and the Rumbia team are fictional characters created for this portfolio demonstration. No awards, reviews or real restaurant claims are presented.
